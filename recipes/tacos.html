Taco Recipe

Who loves tacos? Dragons love tacos. So if you've got a hungry dragon, why not make him some mouth watering tacos?

The key to delicious tacos is to use human meat. There is nothing more satisfying to a dragon, than the succulent flesh of a town villager or inn keeper.

So here it is my dragon pleasing, village terrorizing recipe for tacos. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Human flesh
  • Taco shells
  • Cheese
  • Taco sauce
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Capture villager
  2. Roast them over them over pit of flame
  3. Heat up shells
  4. Eat some cheese while you wait for human to stop screaming
  5. Assemble tacos
